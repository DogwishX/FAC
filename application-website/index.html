About Me

Hi! My name is Patrick Delaney (he/him), I'm currently investing my time and effort into a career I believe is the right one for me.
I started out as a penniless dropout backpacker in Brazil. I had to work at various places, doing different things.
Things changed when I got my first office job in Ireland, at a company that provided services for Microsoft. That's where I discovered I could automate most of my tasks through Python, VBA and accessing their SQL databases. This experience showed me I really enjoy solving problems and building stuff from a blank page. So I recently decided to take the risk, quit my job and study full-time to become a web dev. It is my belief that being accepted and studying under the Founders and Coders curriculum is a step in the right direction to make that happen.
