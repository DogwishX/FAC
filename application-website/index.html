    About Me

    Hi! My name is Patrick Delaney (he/him), I'm currently investing my time and effort into a career I believe is the right one for me.

    I started out as a penniless dropout backpacker in Brazil. I had to work at various places, doing different things.

    Things changed when I got my first office job in Ireland, at a company that provided services for Microsoft. That's where I discovered I could automate most of my tasks through Python, VBA and accessing their SQL databases. This experience showed me I really enjoyed solving problems and building stuff from a blank page. So I recently quit my job, decided to take the risk and study full-time to become a web dev.
